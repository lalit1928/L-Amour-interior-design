Our Story

Design born from darkness

L'Amour Interior Designs emerged from a singular conviction: that truly extraordinary spaces are defined not by what is illuminated, but by what is held in shadow. We work with the full tonal spectrum — from matte obsidian to burnished gold — to craft interiors with genuine emotional weight.

Our team combines traditional craftsmanship with AI-augmented visualisation tools, delivering a process that is both deeply human and breathtakingly precise. We take on fewer than twelve projects annually, ensuring every client receives our complete attention.

Based in India, working globally. From luxury penthouses to boutique hotels, our signature aesthetic adapts to every culture while retaining its essential character: restrained, confident, unforgettable.

Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI image generation work?
+
Our AI uses advanced machine learning models trained on thousands of interior design images. Simply describe your vision, and the AI generates photorealistic renderings in seconds. You can refine and regenerate until you're satisfied.
Can I use the generated designs commercially?
+
Yes, all designs generated through our platform are yours to use. You can use them for personal projects, client presentations, or commercial purposes. We retain no rights to your generated content.
What file formats can I download?
+
Generated images are available in high-resolution PNG format (up to 4K). You can download directly from the platform and use in presentations, mood boards, or print materials.
Do you offer custom design consultations?
+
Yes! Beyond AI generation, we offer bespoke design consultations with our team. Contact us for details on custom projects, material sourcing, and full interior design services.
How accurate are the AI-generated designs?
+
Our AI generates highly realistic renderings that closely match real-world materials, lighting, and proportions. However, actual implementation may vary based on real materials, lighting conditions, and spatial constraints.
Is my API key secure?
+
Yes. Your API key is stored only in your browser's local storage and never transmitted to our servers. All API calls are made directly from your browser to the AI service provider.
Can I save my favorite templates?
+
Absolutely. Click "Use this template" on any design to automatically populate the prompt field. You can then customize it further or generate variations.
What styles can the AI generate?
+
We support multiple styles including Dark Luxury, Minimalist, Cyberpunk, Realistic, Wabi-Sabi, Art Deco, and more. You can also describe custom styles in your prompt.
How do I get started?
+
Sign up for free, get your Gemini API key from aistudio.google.com, and start generating! Our platform is intuitive and requires no design experience.
